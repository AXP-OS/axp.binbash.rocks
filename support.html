Personal support (PM,DM,Mail)
Most likely you ended up here because you tried to contact me personally with a support request.
Of course there may be reasons to communicate with each other personally in private, but usually (and this is why you ended up here) it is rather counterproductive.

So Why?

Just imagine someone asking me the question:
How do I get into recovery mode?
I answer: You have to do this and that.

2 days later, another user writes me:
How do I get into the Recovery Mode?
I answer: You have to do this and that.

again a few days later:
How do I get into the Recovery Mode?
I answer: You have to do this and that.

Does this make sense to you? Likely not as it does not for me. That's why there are forums, where a question can be asked and answered once.
This saves time and nerves, so please stop making private support requests to me and search the forum and if you don't find an answer: just open a new thread.

If you want you can send me the link to your thread via DM/PM but remember, I do this in my spare time so don't expect to get a guaranteed answer in a certain time.

One more thing:
If you have a problem, it is almost always helpful to add pictures, screenshots and log files.
Often a picture says 1000 times more than a long description - even if giving a lot of details is always a good idea ;)
